About Me

Joyce Waandja, also known as Oshoveli, is a dynamic 19-year-old student at the Namibia University of Science and Technology, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Informatics and Computing. With experience in public speaking, she serves as a radio presenter at Uitani Childline Radio and a presenter for the #BeFree Studios. She has represented Namibia on global platforms, including the Woman Deliver Conference 2023 in Kigali, Rwanda. Waandja has also been a panelist at fundraiser gala dinners and a panelist in a high-level discussion on Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) in New York, USA. Driven by a passion for technology, the young lady is also working on innovative projects to support small businesses in Namibia by offering accessible digital solutions. Her work as an ambassador for the #BeFree and #BreakFree campaigns reflects her commitment to creating positive change in her community.
